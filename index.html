History

It all originated from modern quantum theory developed in the 1920s to explain wave-particle duality at atomic scales. This eventually led to the development of digital computers, replacing human computers due to their limitations.

Algorithm

Quantum algorithms like Shor’s and Grover’s provide significant speedups over classical counterparts by utilizing quantum principles such as superposition and entanglement.

Info

Quantum computing is an emerging field poised to revolutionize cryptography, materials science, and complex problem-solving. Tech giants and researchers are rapidly advancing this transformative technology.
